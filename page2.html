types of pollution

fd

There's exist multiple types of pollution, and each one can affect the environment in different ways. Below are the different types of pollution

Air Pollution

Air pollution occurs when harmful gases or particles get in the air causing health problems like asthma and contributes to climate change. Often from things like cars, factories or burning fossil fuels

Water pollution

water pollution is when harmful waste chemicals, or substances enter rivers, lakes or the ocean, making the water unsafe. Often from sewage, oil spills or farming runoff.

Soil/Land pollution

Land pollution is when chemicals, industrial waste and garbage get dumped onto land. This can poison plants animals and even us when it gets into our food and water supplies

Noise Pollution

Noise pollution is caused by loud or constant sounds from traffic, machines, or construction. It may lead to hearing problems, stress, or disturbed sleep.

Light pollution

Light pollution is caused by too many artificial lights that brighten the night sky. This often results in disrupts sleep, wildlife patterns, and can also make it harder to see stars in the night sky.
