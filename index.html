    What is pollution?

    Pollution is when harmful substances or materials are released into the environment, making it dirty or unsafe. It can affect the air we breathe, the water we drink, and the land we live on. Most pollution is caused by human activity, like cars releasing exhaust fumes, factories dumping chemicals, or people littering. This damage doesn’t just hurt nature, it also puts people and animals at risk. Over time, pollution can lead to serious problems like health issues, climate change, and the destruction of ecosystems.
